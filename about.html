About Us

Toronto Cupcakes was founded in 2010 with a passion for handcrafted treats and a dream to spread joy through cupcakes. Since then, we’ve become one of the GTA’s most recognized names for gourmet, freshly baked cupcakes.

Whether you're celebrating a birthday, planning a wedding, or just craving something sweet, we offer a wide variety of flavors, including seasonal favorites and custom logo designs for corporate events. Our cupcakes are made fresh daily using high-quality ingredients and attention to detail.

We’re also proud to support local events and charities, believing that good food should bring people together. From simple pleasures to special occasions, every cupcake we bake is designed to make someone’s day.

About Michelle
Inspired by her love of baking and community, Michelle opened Toronto Cupcakes in 2010. Her goal was simple: to bring joy, one cupcake at a time.
